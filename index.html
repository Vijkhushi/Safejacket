About Us

Safe work industry is a PVT LTD firm. This company came into existence in the February 2017 as one of the leading Manufacturer, Trader, Wholesaler, Distributer and Supplier of an appreciable and durable range of safety products.

Mission

We value construction safety as a core behavior, valuing our employees' safety not only at work, but also at home.

Vision

To become the leading construction firm, while delivering projects that consistently exceed international and provide customer satisfaction.

Motto

Safety Jackets

Traffic Safety

Road traffic safety refers to the methods and measures used to prevent road users from being killed or seriously injured.

Safety Gloves

Safety work gloves are available with padding on the back to minimize the impact of objects falling on your hands.

Safety Goggles

These goggles feature a secure shield around the entire eye that protects against hazards coming from all directions.

Safety Shoes

Shoes are designed to minimise the risk of injury and reduce the severity of any injuries that may occur.

Safety Harness

A safety harness is a form of protective equipment designed to safeguard the user from injury or death from falling.

Disposable Items

Products worn over the nose and mouth that seal tightly to the face in order to filter airborne particles.

Safety Helmet

Safety helmets are designed to protect the head against falling objects .
